Auction FAQs

What do I need to bring on auction day?

Please bring a valid government-issued ID, proof of address, and payment method (cash, credit card, or bank check). If you plan to bid, you'll need to register and receive a bidder number.

How does payment work?

We accept cash, credit cards (with 3% processing fee), and bank checks. A deposit of $500 or 10% (whichever is greater) is due immediately after winning a bid, with full payment required within 48 hours.

Can I inspect vehicles before bidding?

Yes, vehicle inspection is available from 5:30 PM until 6:45 PM on auction day. We encourage all bidders to thoroughly inspect vehicles before bidding.

When do I pick up my vehicle?

Vehicles must be picked up within 3 business days after full payment. We offer secure storage until pickup, but vehicles not picked up after 7 days will incur a daily storage fee of $25.
