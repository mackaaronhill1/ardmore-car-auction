Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our auction process.

When do your auctions take place?

Our regular auctions are held every Monday at 7:00 PM. Doors open at 5:30 PM for registration and vehicle inspection.

Do I need a dealer license to bid?

No, our auctions are open to the public. You do not need a dealer license to participate.

What forms of payment do you accept?

We accept cash, credit cards (with a 3% processing fee), bank checks, and wire transfers.
