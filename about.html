About Ardmore Car Auction

Serving Southern Oklahoma since 1995

Our Story

Established in 1995, Ardmore Car Auction has been a cornerstone of the automotive sales community in Southern Oklahoma for over 30 years. What began as a small family operation has grown into one of the region's most trusted car auction houses.

Located in Overbrook, just outside of Ardmore, our 5-acre facility hosts weekly auctions that draw buyers and sellers from across Oklahoma, Texas, and beyond. Our mission has remained the same since day one: to provide a fair, transparent marketplace where quality vehicles change hands at competitive prices.

Our Mission

Our Facility

Our modern auction facility features:

  • 5-acre lot with space for over 200 vehicles
  • Comfortable indoor auction arena with seating for 100+ bidders
  • On-site inspection area
  • Convenient parking
  • Refreshment area
  • Free Wi-Fi for all attendees

Located just minutes from downtown Ardmore, our facility at 438 Lodge Rd in Overbrook offers a convenient location for buyers and sellers from the surrounding area.
